Your profile is a great way for others to learn more about you before they book your listing or message you. When your profile is robust, it helps others know that you're reliable and authentic to the views of Roomi. Whether you're a lister or a mover, the more complete your profile is, the more views and requests you are likely to receive. It's required that at least one profile photo is of you. No buildings, animals, objects, landscapes, or anything else that isn't you. The only time someone is allowed to use a logo as profile photo on Roomi is an approved partner that is in an active partnership with Roomi. Everyone can use Korshi as a marketplace for building neighbors. It's free to register and list your space. You can list your free space in any of the locations we support. It's free to find a place to rent. We offer additional paid services such as background checks, identity checks, messaging plans, listing credits, profile enhancement, and listing enhancement. After completing the background check, you can let others know that you have passed the background check and have been verified. A listing boost will place your listing at the top of the search results in your region for the duration you choose. This can help your room get exposure if you need to fill it up quickly.
